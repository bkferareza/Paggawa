# LANE-04 - Barangay Quest Board

STATUS: VALIDATED

## Purpose

Create the Paggawa Quest barangay-assisted workspace and local quest board.

## Required Inputs

- Job Requests lane
- Worker Discovery lane
- Product Brief

## Intended Output

Barangay staff can manage assisted requests and worker registry in Paggawa Quest.

Open jobs appear as quest board items.

## In Scope

- Paggawa Quest quest board
- assisted job requests
- worker registry
- simple status updates
- basic complaint/feedback note placeholder

## Out of Scope

- official LGU integration
- legal certification
- full case management
- government identity system
- printed reports

## Validation Criteria

- Barangay staff can view open jobs in Paggawa Quest.
- Barangay staff can create assisted requests.
- Barangay staff can view registered workers.
- Quest board clearly shows open job requests.
