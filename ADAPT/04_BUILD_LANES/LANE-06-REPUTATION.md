# LANE-06 - Reputation

STATUS: VALIDATED

## Purpose

Allow completed work to update worker reputation.

## Required Inputs

- Matching lane
- Domain Model

## Intended Output

Resident can mark a job completed and leave a review in Paggawa Mobile.

Worker profile updates completed job count and rating through the shared data layer.

## In Scope

- mark job completed
- leave rating/review
- completed jobs count
- simple rating display

## Out of Scope

- dispute resolution
- moderation workflow
- appeal process
- complex trust scoring

## Validation Criteria

- Matched job can be marked completed.
- Resident can leave review.
- Worker completed job count updates.
- Worker rating appears on profile.
