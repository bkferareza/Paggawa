# Product Thesis

Paggawa is not merely a booking app.

It is a local trust and discovery layer for skilled work.

It is designed around:

- nearby labor visibility
- nearby job visibility
- community referrals
- barangay-assisted access
- support for non-tech-literate users
- visibility for skilled workers who do not know how to market themselves online

The product is rooted in the Filipino reality that labor discovery often begins with:

> “May kakilala ka ba?”

Paggawa makes that local trust network more visible, structured, and accessible.
